Our Story

Aruba's Favorite
Food Truck

Ask anyone on the island and they'll tell you — Julio's Corner is legendary. Born from a passion for authentic Venezuelan street food, Julio's started in the parking lot of the Sasaki bowling alley and grew into a two-location late-night institution.

Every item on the menu is crafted to deliver the bold, unmistakable flavors of Venezuelan late-night culture: think crispy fried plantain sandwiches, juicy pepitos, smoky grilled meats, and cheese-filled tequeños. Massive portions, fresh ingredients, made-to-order every night.

Locals and tourists alike make it a ritual. Once you try it, you'll come back every trip.

What We Serve

The Menu

Venezuelan street food done right. Everything is made fresh, every night. Bold combinations you won't find anywhere else on the island.

🧀

Tequeños

The quintessential Venezuelan snack. Crispy fried dough wrapped around melty white cheese, served with garlic, tartar & rosada dipping sauces.

8 pieces · $8
🍌

Patacón

Two crispy fried plantain slabs as the "bread," loaded with chicken or pulled beef, lettuce, corn, bacon, cheese & house sauces. Try El Carajito.

From $11
🌮

Tacos

Venezuelan-style tacos with soft or hard tortillas, beef or chicken, lettuce, corn, pico de gallo & parmesan dust. Served with fries.

From $10.50
🥪

Pepito

The classic Venezuelan sandwich — loaded with grilled meat, avocado, sauces and toppings. A cult favorite among regulars.
